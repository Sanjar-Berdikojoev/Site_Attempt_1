About us

First Class Driving School has been a pioneer in the field of driving training since 2015. Driven by world-class technology & training curriculum, First Class Driving School has elevated and redefined the standards of driving training in the country. From the day the iconic Maruti 800 was launched in 1983, First Class has been spearheading a revolution of change - turning an entire country’s need for driving into its love for driving. Heralding an automotive revolution over the years, First Class has pioneered category-defining design, technology and experience. From the Maruti Zen, Esteem, Omni to today’s Swift, Brezza, Baleno, and Ciaz - First Class has redefined the concept of automotive design.

Here's what you can come to expect as a student of First Class Driving School:

  • Proficient trainig experts
  • Practical learning through state-of-the-art technology
  • Educative theoretical lessons
