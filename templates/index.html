First Class Driving School

If you want to get a driver's license and are looking for a driving school that is interesting and enjoyable to attend, you are in the right place! We are not bored in theory and comfortable in driving! We are a driving school where we taught how to drive a vehicle and helped hundreds of people in Kyrgyzstan get their driving license.

Why us?

Own autodrome, located 5 minutes drive from the driving school, with excellent asphalt surface, with lighting for night use, allows you to train driving vehicles of categories "A", "B", "C", "D". The possibility of changing the master of industrial driving training in case of your psychological incompatibility with him. A variety of forms of training: morning and evening groups on weekdays, weekend driving school, intensive training groups, individual training for cadets, which will allow you to choose driving courses based on your employment and psychological orientation. 32 modern vehicles of domestic and foreign production of category A, B, C, D. Friendly and caring attitude towards students from all employees of the driving school. First Class will make your driving experience comfortable and easy! First Class offers a flexible driving instruction schedule that instructors will adapt to the needs of the cadets of each group for your convenience.

Our politics

Accounting for class attendance, academic performance and topics covered is maintained by teachers and masters of industrial training in the relevant accounting documentation. The duration of an academic hour of theoretical and practical classes is 1 academic hour (45 minutes), and for driving lessons - 1 astronomical hour (60 minutes), including the time for debriefing, paperwork. Theoretical and practical training is carried out in equipped classrooms using educational and methodological and visual aids in accordance with the List of training materials for driver training. In the course of practical training in the subject "First Aid", students should be able to perform techniques for providing first aid (self-help) to victims on the roads. Driving training is carried out outside the grid of training time by the master of industrial training individually with each trainee in accordance with the schedule of the order of driving training (on a simulator and a training vehicle). At the same time, the master can train up to four trainees simultaneously on the simulator (according to the number of training places), and one trainee on the training vehicle. Driving instruction consists of initial driving instruction and practical driving instruction on training routes in real traffic conditions.

Our goals

The aim of our driving school is to train candidates for ABCD category drivers to confident, accident-free driving in heavy urban traffic. For your comfortable learning, successful passing of the exam in the traffic police and subsequent safe driving behavior, First School Driving auto school has created all the necessary conditions.

Our courses

Since 2003, the driving school has been a member of the ASSOCIATION OF LEGAL ENTITIES "ASSOCIATION OF DRIVING SCHOOLS AND MOTORISTS", since 2004 it has joined the International Association, which characterizes the school as having reached a high level of development and modernization. Every year, First School participates in international conferences on road safety, on the latest technologies for training new generation drivers. In 2012, the driving school was awarded a diploma for success in driver training and road safety. This is an impressive figure: out of 6,000 schools in Kyrgyzstan, 35 were marked, including First School Driving School. All this time, the main principles in our work were: Safety, Culture, Professionalism. These are our components that unite our past and present together. To date, First School is a decent, in terms of the level of teaching staff and training of the driver's staff of various categories, a driving school. For 20 years of its activity, the driving school has been constantly participating in all advanced events of the Kyrgyz scale for the exchange of experience - it shares its positive developments, adopts the positive aspects of the activities of other driving schools.
